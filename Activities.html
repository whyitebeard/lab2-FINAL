ACTIVITY 3

1000 Floor Elevator

Given this opportunity to construct an exclusive elevator which would cater one thousand floors, I hereby declare my own solution for this task which includes the following details:


- Dedicated emergency button and interface
- Built-in snack store
- Air-conditioned
- 30 available seats
- Air freshener (System base)
- Precise Estimated Time of Arrival
- Equipped with fast transportation system
- Divided into 100 floors (10 elevators)

Does these features will greatly benefit the client from its need?
Assurance is given, due to security system which directly coordinates with the guard/personnel for any in great need of assistance (failure of power supply, stuck on a floor, natural calamities, and the likes). In addition, defense security is equipped for any unexpected external damage which will automatically trigger the system to exceed its structure by fortifying the metals, cables, etc. of the elevator.

These equipment are technologically advanced, simply saying they are developed to furtherly reduce the accidents that may occur. Also, on the internal emergencies, there is the interface that directly communicates with the security administration for them to give further assistance and solve any issues that may arise. If the issue wasn’t solved, the security has to instruct users to press the red button to immediately stop the operation of the elevator. This allows the individuals to exit the elevator on a specific floor that ensures the safest route available.

Moving forward, my next premise will be the cleanliness of the elevators’ air is minutely monitored by the air-vent system which was invented to prevent and eliminate unwanted smell that can cause inconvenience to the users. The system will check if there are any anomalies within the air, if proven it would give warning to the individuals that air freshener will be sprayed within 20 seconds. But, air freshener will also spray the place for every 30 minutes.
Knowing that there are 1000 floors and it is equally divided to 100 per elevator, the structure also includes selling of snacks inside the elevator to cater the hungry individuals. Employees can pay the product/s through their ID validation using their unique ID number and would be deducted on their salary.

Last but not least, is the speed of transportation. I, in behalf of my colleagues, would like present the future of elevators, “The Mayt”. This system allows to greatly reduce the time spent by the employees on waiting for their destination. How? System, will teleport the elevator on the in queue floors. But, there will be specific range of floors that can only be teleported to. For example, if you are on floor 13 and you’ll go to floor 15 in between no individuals pressed the button for the elevator on floor 14 then you’ll be regularly transported without teleportation. If you were on floor 30 and pressed floor 50 which there were no individuals pressed the button in between 31 and 49 you’ll be automatically teleported. These are the conditions, simply saying there are regular and teleport system that will be implemented.
